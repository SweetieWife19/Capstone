WELCOME TO BAGUIO CITY!

Baguio City is a vibrant and picturesque destination nestled in the mountainous region of the Philippines. It boasts of its natural beauty and offers a variety of outdoor activities for tourists to enjoy. Take a moment to explore its charming attractions and don't hesitate to immerse yourself in its unique culture.

Strawberries

Strawberries are a common fruit in Baguio and are often found in the city's markets and fruit stands. They are typically in season from December to May, but can also be found year-round. These delicious red berries are known for their sweet and slightly tangy taste, making them a favorite among locals and tourists alike. In Baguio, strawberries are often used in a variety of ways, including in jams, desserts, and even as a topping for pancakes and waffles. Many visitors to the city also enjoy picking their own strawberries at local farms, which is a popular activity during the harvest season.
